Characters often find coins on their adventures and can spend those
coins in shops, inns, and other businesses. Coins come in different
denominations based on the relative worth of their material. The Coin
Values table lists coins and how much they're

worth relative to the Gold Piece, which is the game's main coin. For
example, 100 Copper Pieces are worth 1 Gold Piece.
