## Lifestyle Expenses

Lifestyle expenses summarize the cost of living in a fantasy world. They
cover lodging, food, equipment maintenance, and other necessities.

At the start of each week or month (GM's choice), choose a lifestyle
below---Wretched, Squalid, Poor, Modest, Comfortable, Wealthy, or
Aristocratic---and pay the price to sustain that lifestyle.

Lifestyles have no inherent consequences, but the GM might take them
into account when determining risks or how others perceive your
character.

### [Wretched (Free)]{.underline} 

You survive via chance and charity. You're often exposed to natural
dangers as a result of sleeping outside.

###  [Squalid (1 SP per Day)]{.underline} 

You spend the bare minimum for your necessities. You might be exposed to
unhealthy conditions and opportunistic criminals.

### [Poor (2 SP per Day)]{.underline} 

You spend frugally for your necessities.

### [Modest (1 GP per Day)]{.underline} 

You support yourself at an average level.

### [Comfortable (2 GP per Day)]{.underline} 

You spend modestly for your necessities and enjoy a few luxuries.

### [Wealthy (4 GP per Day)]{.underline} 

You're accustomed to the finer things in life and might have servants.

### [Aristocratic (10 GP per Day)]{.underline} 

You pay for the best and might have a staff that sup- ports your
lifestyle. Others notice your wealth and might encourage you to share
it, either legally or otherwise.
