##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Diabolical Restoration.*** If the erinyes dies outside the Nine
> Hells, its body disappears in sulfurous smoke, and it gains a new body
> instantly, reviving with all its Hit Points somewhere in the Nine
> Hells.
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The erinyes has Advantage on saving throws
> against spells and other magical effects.
>
> ***Magic Rope.*** The erinyes has a magic rope. While bear- ing it,
> the erinyes can use the Entangling Rope action. The rope has AC 20, HP
> 90, and Immunity to Poison and Psychic damage. The rope turns to dust
> if reduced to 0 Hit Points, if it is 5+ feet away from the erinyes
>
> for 1 hour or more, or if the erinyes dies. If the rope is damaged or
> destroyed, the erinyes can fully restore it when finishing a Short or
> Long Rest.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Multiattack.*** The erinyes makes three Withering Sword attacks and
> can use Entangling Rope.
>
> ***Withering Sword.** Melee Attack Roll:* +8, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 13
> (2d8 + 4) Slashing damage plus 11 (2d10) Ne- crotic damage.
>
> ***Entangling Rope (Requires Magic Rope).** Strength Saving Throw:* DC
> 16, one creature the erinyes can see within 120 feet. *Failure:* 14
> (4d6) Force damage, and the target has the Restrained condition until
> the rope is de- stroyed, the erinyes uses a Bonus Action to release
> the target, or the erinyes uses Entangling Rope again.

##### [Reactions]{.underline} 

> ***Parry.** Trigger:* The erinyes is hit by a melee attack roll while
> holding a weapon. *Response:* The erinyes adds 4 to its AC against
> that attack, possibly causing it to miss.
