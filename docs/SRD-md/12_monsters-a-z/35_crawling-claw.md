> Understands Common but can't speak **CR** 3 (XP 700; PB +2)

##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Swarm.*** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice
> versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a
> Tiny creature. The swarm can't regain Hit Points or gain Temporary Hit
> Points.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Swarm of Grasping Hands.** Melee Attack Roll:* +4, reach 5 ft.
> *Hit:* 20 (4d8 + 2) Necrotic damage, or 11 (2d8 + 2) Necrotic damage
> if the swarm is Bloodied. If the target is a Medium or smaller
> creature, it has the Prone condition.
