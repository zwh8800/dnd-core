> **Skills** Athletics +9, Perception +3
>
> **Immunities** Cold
>
> **Senses** Passive Perception 13
>
> **Languages** Giant
>
> **CR** 8 (XP 3,900; PB +3)

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Multiattack.*** The giant makes two attacks, using Frost Axe or
> Great Bow in any combination.
>
> ***Frost Axe.** Melee Attack Roll:* +9, reach 10 ft. *Hit:* 19 (2d12 +
> 6) Slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) Cold damage.
>
> ***Great Bow.** Ranged Attack Roll:* +9, range 150/600 ft. *Hit:* 17
> (2d10 + 6) Piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) Cold damage, and the target's
> Speed decreases by 10 feet until the end of its next turn.

##### [Bonus Actions]{.underline} 

> ***War Cry (Recharge 5--6).*** The giant or one creature of its choice
> that can see or hear it gains 16 (2d10 + 5) Temporary Hit Points and
> has Advantage on attack rolls until the start of the giant's next
> turn.
