##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Rend.** Melee Attack Roll:* +4, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2)
> Piercing damage.
>
> ***Bone Bow.** Ranged Attack Roll:* +3, range 150/600 ft.
>
> *Hit:* 6 (1d10 + 1) Piercing damage.

##### [Bonus Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Rampage (1/Day).*** Immediately after dealing damage to a creature
> that is already Bloodied, the gnoll moves up to half its Speed, and it
> makes one Rend attack.
