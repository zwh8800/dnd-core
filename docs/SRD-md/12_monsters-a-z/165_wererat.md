##### [Bonus Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Shape-Shift.*** The wererat shape-shifts into a Medium rat-humanoid
> hybrid or a Small rat, or it returns to its true humanoid form. Its
> game statistics, other than its size, are the same in each form. Any
> equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed.
