> **Senses** Darkvision 60 ft.; Passive Perception 11
>
> **Languages** None
>
> **CR** 1/2 (XP 100; PB +2)

##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Iron Scent.*** The rust monster can pinpoint the location of
> ferrous metal within 30 feet of itself.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Multiattack.*** The rust monster makes one Bite attack and uses
> Antennae twice.
>
> ***Bite.** Melee Attack Roll:* +3, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 5 (1d8 + 1)
> Piercing damage.
>
> ***Antennae.*** The rust monster targets one nonmagical metal
> object---armor or a weapon---worn or carried by a creature within 5
> feet of itself. *Dexterity Saving Throw:* DC 11, the creature with the
> object. *Failure:* The object takes a −1 penalty to the AC it offers
> (ar- mor) or to its attack rolls (weapon). Armor is destroyed if the
> penalty reduces its AC to 10, and a weapon is destroyed if its penalty
> reaches −5. The penalty can be removed by casting the *Mending* spell
> on the armor
>
> or weapon.
>
> ***Destroy Metal.*** The rust monster touches a nonmagi- cal metal
> object within 5 feet of itself that isn't being worn or carried. The
> touch destroys a 1-foot Cube of the object.

##### [Reactions]{.underline} 

> ***Reflexive Antennae.** Trigger:* An attack roll hits the rust
> monster. *Response:* The rust monster uses Antennae.
