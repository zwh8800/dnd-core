##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Pack Tactics.*** The hound has Advantage on an attack roll against
> a creature if at least one of the hound's al- lies is within 5 feet of
> the creature and the ally doesn't have the Incapacitated condition.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Multiattack.*** The hound makes two Bite attacks.
>
> ***Bite.** Melee Attack Roll:* +5, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 7 (1d8 + 3)
> Piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) Fire damage.
>
> ***Fire Breath (Recharge 5--6).** Dexterity Saving Throw:* DC 12, each
> creature in a 15-foot Cone. *Failure:* 17 (5d6) Fire damage.
> *Success:* Half damage.
