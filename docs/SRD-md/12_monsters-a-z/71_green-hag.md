##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Amphibious.*** The hag can breathe air and water.
>
> ***Coven Magic.*** While within 30 feet of at least two hag allies,
> the hag can cast one of the following spells, requiring no Material
> components, using the spell's normal casting time, and using
> Intelligence as the spell- casting ability (spell save DC 11):
> *Augury*, *Find Familiar*, *Identify*, *Locate Object*, *Scrying*, or
> *Unseen Servant*.
>
> The hag must finish a Long Rest before using this trait to
>
> cast that spell again.
>
> ***Mimicry.*** The hag can mimic animal sounds and hu- manoid voices.
> A creature that hears the sounds can tell they are imitations only
> with a successful DC 14 Wisdom (Insight) check.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Multiattack.*** The hag makes two Claw attacks.

> ***Claw.** Melee Attack Roll:* +6, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 4)
> Slashing damage plus 3 (1d6) Poison damage.
>
> ***Spellcasting.*** The hag casts one of the following spells,
> requiring no Material components and using Wisdom
>
> as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 12, +4 to hit with spell
> attacks):
>
> **At Will:** *Dancing Lights*, *Disguise Self* (24-hour du- ration),

> Invisible), *Minor Illusion*, *Ray of Sickness* (level 3 version)
