##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Fire Aura.*** At the end of each of the salamander's turns, each
> creature of the salamander's choice in a 5-foot Emanation originating
> from the salamander takes 7 (2d6) Fire damage.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Multiattack.*** The salamander makes two Flame Spear attacks. It
> can replace one attack with a use of Constrict.
>
> ***Flame Spear.** Melee or Ranged Attack Roll:* +7, reach 5 ft. or
> range 20/60 ft. *Hit:* 13 (2d8 + 4) Piercing damage plus 7 (2d6) Fire
> damage. *Hit or Miss:* The spear magi- cally returns to the
> salamander's hand immediately after a ranged attack.
>
> ***Constrict.** Strength Saving Throw:* DC 15, one Large or smaller
> creature the salamander can see within 10 feet. *Failure:* 11 (2d6 +
> 4) Bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) Fire damage. The target has the
> Grappled condition (es- cape DC 14), and it has the Restrained
> condition until the grapple ends.
