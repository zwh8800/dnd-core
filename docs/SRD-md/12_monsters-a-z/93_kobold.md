##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Pack Tactics.*** The kobold has Advantage on an attack roll against
> a creature if at least one of the kobold's al- lies is within 5 feet
> of the creature and the ally doesn't have the Incapacitated condition.
>
> ***Sunlight Sensitivity.*** While in sunlight, the kobold has
> Disadvantage on ability checks and attack rolls.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Dagger.** Melee or Ranged Attack Roll:* +4, reach 5 ft. or range
> 20/60 ft. *Hit:* 4 (1d4 + 2) Piercing damage.
