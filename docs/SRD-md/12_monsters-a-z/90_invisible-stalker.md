##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Air Form.*** The stalker can enter an enemy's space and stop there.
> It can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch without expending
> extra movement to do so.
>
> ***Invisibility.*** The stalker has the Invisible condition.
