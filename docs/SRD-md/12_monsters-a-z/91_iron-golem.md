##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Fire Absorption.*** Whenever the golem is subjected to Fire damage,
> it regains a number of Hit Points equal to the Fire damage dealt.
>
> ***Immutable Form.*** The golem can't shape-shift.
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The golem has Advantage on saving throws
> against spells and other magical effects.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Multiattack.*** The golem makes two attacks, using Bladed Arm or
> Fiery Bolt in any combination.
>
> ***Bladed Arm.** Melee Attack Roll:* +12, reach 10 ft. *Hit:* 20
> (3d8 + 7) Slashing damage plus 10 (3d6) Fire damage.
>
> ***Fiery Bolt.** Ranged Attack Roll:* +10, range 120 ft. *Hit:*
>
> 36 (8d8) Fire damage.
>
> ***Poison Breath (Recharge 6).** Constitution Saving Throw:* DC 18,
> each creature in a 60-foot Cone. *Fail- ure:* 55 (10d10) Poison
> damage. *Success:* Half damage.
