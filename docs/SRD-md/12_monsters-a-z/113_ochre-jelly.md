> **Resistances** Acid
>
> **Immunities** Lightning, Slashing; Charmed, Deafened, Exhaustion,
> Frightened, Grappled, Prone, Restrained
>
> **Senses** Blindsight 60 ft.; Passive Perception 8
>
> **Languages** None
>
> **CR** 2 (XP 450; PB +2)

##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Amorphous.*** The jelly can move through a space as narrow as 1
> inch without expending extra move- ment to do so.
>
> ***Spider Climb.*** The jelly can climb difficult surfaces, including
> along ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Pseudopod.** Melee Attack Roll:* +4, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 12 (3d6 +
> 2) Acid damage.

#####  [Reactions]{.underline} 

> ***Split.** Trigger:* While the jelly is Large or Medium and has 10+
> Hit Points, it becomes Bloodied or is subjected to Lightning or
> Slashing damage. *Response:* The jelly splits into two new **Ochre
> Jellies**. Each new jelly is one size smaller than the original jelly
> and acts on its Initia- tive. The original jelly's Hit Points are
> divided evenly between the new jellies (round down).
