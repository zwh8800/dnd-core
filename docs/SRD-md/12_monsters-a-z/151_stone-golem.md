##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Immutable Form.*** The golem can't shape-shift.
>
> ***Magic Resistance.*** The golem has Advantage on saving throws
> against spells and other magical effects.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Multiattack.*** The golem makes two attacks, using Slam or Force
> Bolt in any combination.
>
> ***Slam.** Melee Attack Roll:* +10, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 15 (2d8
>
> \+ 6) Bludgeoning damage plus 9 (2d8) Force damage.
>
> ***Force Bolt.** Ranged Attack Roll:* +9, range 120 ft. *Hit:*
>
> 22 (4d10) Force damage.

##### [Bonus Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Slow (Recharge 5--6).*** The golem casts the *Slow* spell,
> requiring no spell components and using Constitution as the
> spellcasting ability (spell save DC 17).
