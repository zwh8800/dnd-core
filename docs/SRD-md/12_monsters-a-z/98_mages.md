##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Multiattack.*** The mage makes three Arcane Burst attacks.
>
> ***Arcane Burst.** Melee or Ranged Attack Roll:* +6, reach 5 ft. or
> range 120 ft. *Hit:* 16 (3d8 + 3) Force damage.
>
> ***Spellcasting.*** The mage casts one of the following spells, using
> Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 14):
>
> **At Will:** *Detect Magic*, *Light*, *Mage Armor* (included in AC),
> *Mage Hand*, *Prestidigitation*
>
> **2/Day Each:** *Fireball* (level 4 version), *Invisibility*
>
> **1/Day Each:** *Cone of Cold*, *Fly*

##### [Bonus Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Misty Step (3/Day).*** The mage casts *Misty Step*, using the same
> spellcasting ability as Spellcasting.

##### [Reactions]{.underline} 

> ***Protective Magic (3/Day).*** The mage casts *Counterspell* or
> *Shield* in response to the spell's trigger, using the same
> spellcasting ability as Spellcasting.
