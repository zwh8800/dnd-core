##### [Traits]{.underline} 

> ***Incorporeal Movement.*** The wraith can move through other
> creatures and objects as if they were Difficult Terrain. It takes 5
> (1d10) Force damage if it ends its turn inside an object.
>
> ***Sunlight Sensitivity.*** While in sunlight, the wraith has
> Disadvantage on ability checks and attack rolls.

##### [Actions]{.underline} 

> ***Life Drain.** Melee Attack Roll:* +6, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 21 (4d8 +
> 3) Necrotic damage. If the target is a creature, its Hit Point maximum
> decreases by an amount equal to the damage taken.
>
> ***Create Specter.*** The wraith targets a Humanoid corpse within 10
> feet of itself that has been dead for no longer than 1 minute. The
> target's spirit rises as a **Specter** in the space of its corpse or
> in the nearest unoccupied space. The specter is under the wraith's
> control. The wraith can have no more than seven specters under its
> control at a time.
