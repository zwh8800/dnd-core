> attack with a Talons attack.
>

>

>
> ***Talons.** Melee Attack Roll:* +13, reach 5 ft. Hit: 23 (4d6
>

> has the Grappled condition (escape DC 19) from both talons, and it has
> the Restrained condition until the grapple ends.
